Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Maya Homoeopathic Clinic handles information shared through this website.

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Information you choose to share

If you use the appointment request form, the website may open WhatsApp with the details you entered so you can send them to the clinic. Please do not include sensitive diagnosis details or information that is not necessary for arranging contact.

The clinic may receive your name, mobile number, preferred date and time, patient status, consultation type, and short message when you choose to contact it.

How information is used

Information is used to respond to appointment requests, discuss availability, provide directions, or answer questions you send to MHC. The website does not claim to store appointment form data on its own server.

Third-party services

Links to Google Maps, the Google Business Profile, and WhatsApp are provided for convenience. When you follow those links, the relevant third party’s terms and privacy policy apply. An embedded map may load content from Google when you choose to view it.

Cookies and analytics

This static website does not set advertising cookies or include an analytics platform by default. If analytics or other third-party tools are added later, this policy should be updated before publishing.

Contact

For questions about information shared with the clinic, contact MHC at +91 77427 50957.
